KashKitty.
KashKitty is a cute gamifed budgeting app for young girls. It allows the user to keep track of their monthly budgets and transactions. The user's money spending habits will determine the behaviour of the cats.
github
devpost
This project won 1st place for "Best Hack for Educating Young Women on Finance" at SheHacks 2021.
I worked in a team with Rachel Pun (product designer) + 2 other developers. I was the only developer with experience in developing apps so I was the lead frontend developer. I developed: The navigation of the entire app. The async storage of the app to keep track of transactions and user/budget objects . The logic behind the behaviour of the cats and when they appear or disappear depending on user input. The "add budget" popup and functionality.
