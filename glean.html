Glean.
Glean is a gamified mobile app to encourage gleaning by allowing users to schedule trips to local farms and collect leftover crops to donate to food banks or communities in need. Users collect achievements by gleaning.
github
devpost
This project won 2nd place overall at HackCU 2021.
I worked in a team with Rachel Pun (product designer) , Andrew Choi (backend developer) , and Jacob Nishimura (fullstack developer) . I was the lead frontend developer. I developed: The integration with the APIs. The login and sign up screens. The homepage and farm screens. The profile screen with achievements and schedules. The log harvest flow.
